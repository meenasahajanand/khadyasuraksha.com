The game is set in a world where different slime tribes are fighting for control of resources and to become the dominant species. Players take on the role of a slime commander who must lead their armies to victory by strategic planning and using the best available resources.

In the game, players fight battles with other slime tribes to gain territory and loot valuable resources. These battles are immersive and exciting, offering players the opportunity to use the tactical skills they have gathered and putting their strategies to the test. Players aim to amass the greatest and most powerful army that will help them to become the top ruler in the slime kingdom.

Legend of Slime is a game that allows players to connect with like-minded individuals and form alliances. Players can join forces with other commanders to form a tribe, which increases their strength exponentially. Each tribe is situated on a world map that represents the overall state of the game, displaying territories belonging to each tribe.

The game also features different challenges and tournaments that players can compete in and win. These challenges are an excellent way for players to earn valuable resources and show their tactical prowess. Additionally, players can compete with other commanders in tournaments where they battle it out on an even playing field.

The gameplay of Legend of Slime: Idle RPG War game is a unique blend of strategy and adventure elements. Players need to collect different resources to create an army of powerful slimes, each with their unique strengths and weaknesses. Once players have gathered enough resources and raised their slimes army, they can engage in battles with other players and slime tribes to progress in the game.

Players start by collecting slime fragments, which they can use to create their first slime. As the game progresses, players can unlock different slime types like Fire, Water, Electric, Poison, and more by completing different levels and tasks in the game. Players need to upgrade and train their slimes and select the correct slime combinations to ensure that their army is powerful enough to win battles without their direct input.

In an idle game, the player progresses even when they are not online, which allows users to enjoy the game even while they are not actively playing. Players can check-in regularly to upgrade their armies, collect resources, and participate in battles when they have time.

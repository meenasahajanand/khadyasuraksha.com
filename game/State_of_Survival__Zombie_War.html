Description

"State of Survival: Zombie War is a popular mobile strategy game developed by KingsGroup Holdings. It combines elements of strategy, base building, and zombie survival in a post-apocalyptic setting. Released in 2019, the game has gained a substantial player base and has been praised for its engaging gameplay and immersive world.

In State of Survival: Zombie War, players find themselves in a world devastated by a zombie outbreak. As one of the few survivors, their primary objective is to establish and defend a settlement, gather resources, recruit allies, and fight against hordes of infected zombies.

The game offers a compelling storyline that unfolds through a series of quests and missions. Players encounter various characters, each with their own unique backgrounds and abilities, as they progress through the game. These characters play significant roles in the narrative and offer assistance, guidance, and quests to the player.

At the heart of State of Survival: Zombie War is the strategic aspect of base building and resource management. Players start with a small settlement and must expand it by constructing and upgrading buildings, such as resource farms, training facilities, hospitals, and defensive structures. Each building serves a specific purpose and contributes to the overall progress and well-being of the settlement.

Resources play a vital role in the game, as they are required for construction, research, training troops, and various other activities. Players must gather resources such as food, wood, metal, and gas from different sources, including resource plots, abandoned buildings, and by sending out teams of survivors on resource-gathering missions. Managing and balancing resource production and consumption is crucial for the growth and survival of the settlement.

State of Survival: Zombie War features a comprehensive research system that allows players to unlock new technologies and upgrades. Researching new technologies improves resource production, enhances combat capabilities, and unlocks new buildings and units. Players can prioritize their research based on their preferred playstyle, focusing on areas such as combat, resource management, or base defense.
